Without proper community association bookkeeping, boards risk inaccurate budgets, missed compliance deadlines, and potential legal exposure. Our tax preparation for associations service ensures the right forms are filed on time with all applicable deductions applied correctly.
Most community associations benefit from monthly HOA bookkeeping reports, including a balance sheet, income statement, and reserve fund summary. We provide these reports on a regular schedule that works for your board's meeting calendar.
A reserve study analyzes your association's major common elements, estimates their remaining useful life and replacement cost, and recommends annual reserve contributions to ensure funds are available when needed. Reserve study support from our team means your HOA budget planning incorporates accurate reserve funding levels so your community is never caught underfunded for major repairs or replacements.

Compliance Reporting

HOA Compliance Reporting

Staying compliant with DC HOA regulations, governing documents, and IRS requirements is non-negotiable for community associations. Our HOA compliance reporting service ensures that all required financial disclosures, annual reports, and regulatory filings are completed accurately and submitted on time. We monitor deadlines, maintain documentation, and prepare clear compliance summaries that your board can present to members with confidence. Financial Advisory

Expert HOA Financial Advisor

Beyond routine bookkeeping, many boards benefit from strategic financial guidance when making decisions about assessments, capital projects, reserve funding, or budget adjustments. Our expert HOA financial advisor service gives your board access to experienced advisors who can model financial scenarios, evaluate options, and recommend the most fiscally sound path forward for your community. Whether you are planning a major capital improvement or reviewing your association's long-term financial strategy, our HOA financial advisors are ready to help.

Financial Management

Community Financial Management

Our community financial management service is a comprehensive, end-to-end solution for associations that need a fully managed financial operation. We oversee accounts payable and receivable, dues collection tracking, vendor payment processing, bank reconciliations, and financial reporting in an integrated system designed for community associations. As your dedicated community financial management partner, we bring structure, accountability, and transparency to your association's finances every single month.

Financial Statements

Association Financial Statements

Clear, accurate association financial statements are the backbone of sound HOA governance. Our team prepares monthly and annual financial statements that include a balance sheet, income and expense report, reserve fund balance report, and budget variance analysis. These statements are formatted for board presentations, owner disclosure, and auditor review. Reliable association financial statements give your board the information needed to make confident decisions and demonstrate fiscal responsibility to your community.

Budget Planning

HOA Budget Planning and Reserve Study Support

A well-structured annual budget is essential for stable HOA operations, and reserve study support ensures your long-term capital needs are properly funded. Our HOA budget planning service helps boards build realistic operating budgets that reflect actual costs, projected increases, and reserve contribution requirements. Combined with reserve study support, we give your board a complete financial roadmap for both short-term management and multi-year planning. Avoid special assessments and financial surprises with thorough HOA budget planning from our team.

Tax Preparation

Tax Preparation for Associations

HOA tax filings require specialized knowledge that goes beyond standard corporate tax preparation. Our tax preparation for associations service covers all applicable federal filings, including IRS Form 1120-H for qualifying associations, as well as any District of Columbia tax requirements. We review your association's income sources, apply all eligible deductions, and ensure every return is filed accurately and on time. Annual tax preparation for associations is included in our full service packages or available as a standalone engagement for existing clients.
